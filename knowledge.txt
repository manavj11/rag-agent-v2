Company Name: LuminaGrid Technologies

Overview:
LuminaGrid Technologies is a fictional B2B software company founded in 2021. The company builds AI-powered energy optimization platforms for commercial buildings and industrial facilities. Its mission is to reduce energy waste while improving operational efficiency.

Business Model:
LuminaGrid operates on a SaaS subscription model. Customers pay an annual license fee based on the number of buildings monitored and the volume of energy data processed. Enterprise customers may also purchase custom integrations and premium analytics modules.

Products:
1. GridSight Core – A real-time energy monitoring dashboard that aggregates data from smart meters, HVAC systems, and IoT sensors.
2. GridSight Predict – A machine learning module that forecasts energy demand and recommends optimization strategies.
3. GridControl API – An API that allows customers to integrate LuminaGrid’s insights into their existing building management systems.

Target Customers:
LuminaGrid primarily serves:
- Commercial real estate firms
- Manufacturing plants
- Data centers
- University campuses
Customers are typically mid-sized to large organizations with high energy consumption.

Competitive Advantage:
LuminaGrid differentiates itself through:
- Real-time anomaly detection
- Industry-specific ML models
- Fast deployment with minimal hardware changes
- Transparent pricing with no long-term lock-in contracts

Revenue Streams:
- Annual SaaS subscriptions
- Usage-based fees for advanced analytics
- Professional services for onboarding and customization

Company Culture:
LuminaGrid values sustainability, transparency, and practical AI. The company emphasizes explainable machine learning models rather than black-box systems.

LuminaGrid Technologies is a fictional company created for demonstration and training purposes only.
